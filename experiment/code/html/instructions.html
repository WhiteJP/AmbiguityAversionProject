Instructions

As mentioned, this experiment involves making choices between different options. However, unlike many studies which ask you to make a choice about what you would do in a certain situation, this experiment asks you to make a choice about which hypothetical situation you would rather be in.

Here's how it will work in a little more depth. You will be presented with a short, life-like scenario which we call a 'vignette'. After reading this vignette, you will be shown two different hypothetical situations that are related to it. You need to tell us which of these two situations you would rather be in. To do so, you will answer along a scale from "I would definitely rather be in situation A" to "I would definitely rather be in situation B" with "I am indifferent about which situation I would rather be in" in the middle.

Lastly, as we promised, no matter your answers to the following questions, you'll be guaranteed to receive the $0.XX that you signed up for.
