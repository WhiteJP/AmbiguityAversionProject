There is a rare bird, one of the only ones of its kind, that has been spotted in both X and Y. The bird cannot be in two places at once and you do not have time to go to both.

Which of the following situations would you prefer to be in?
A. The rare bird is at either preserve X or preserve Y, but the exact probability for each is unknown.
B. There is a 50% chance that the rare bird is at preserve X, and a 50% chance that the rare bird is at preserve Y.
