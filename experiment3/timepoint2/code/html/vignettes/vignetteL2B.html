Question V_NUM

You find one of these people, person X, extremely unattractive; while the other one, person Y, is of average attractiveness.

Which of the following situations would you rather be in?
A. Either person X or person Y is your date, but the exact probability for each is unknown.
B. There is a 50% chance that person X is your date, and a 50% chance that person Y is your date.
